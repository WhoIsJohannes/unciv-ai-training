Now the medium-rung arm, one micro-batch chunk (--micro-batch-steps 256, N=2400 → 10 chunks): chunk [512:768] → forward_chunk_fn(512,768) = net({k: v[512:768] for k,v in inputs.items()}) → tl,pl,val for 256 rows → _ac_loss_terms(…, adv[512:768], ret[512:768], old_logp[512:768], …) → loss_c = (pol_c + 0.5*val_c − 0.01*ent_c) * (256/2400) → loss_c.backward() (grad accumulates, 256-row graph freed before the next chunk — this is the OOM relief).
