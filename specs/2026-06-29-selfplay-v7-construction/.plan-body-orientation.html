The ask: give the self-play policy its first per-entity control lever — per-city production (what each city builds) — on top of the two civ-global heads (tech, policy), and measure whether controlling production moves the Medium win-rate toward/past the 50% break-even.

How this solves it: a per-city construction head reads the existing (un-pooled) own_cities token embeddings and emits per-city logits over the building∪unit vocabulary; the civ-turn hook pre-fills each deciding city's construction queue (mirroring the tech pattern), records two variable per-city blocks aligned to own_cities, and the trainer adds the per-city construction log-prob into the existing joint PPO ratio. A --control-construction {on,off} flag makes OFF a proven no-op (== v6). A 4-arm experiment (small/medium rung × OFF/ON, replay K=4) isolates construction as the only variable.
